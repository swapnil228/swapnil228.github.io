Design of SFT and RLHF tools for Gen AI use-cases

TELUS Digital

INTRODUCTION

To build Gen AI/LLM apps, we need to do fine tuning of models and apply reinforcement learning with human feedback (RLHF) to improve the quality of the output from the models and align to the defined policies. For doing these processes, we needed to design a set of tools that allow expert annotators to provide prompt response pairs, feedback and rating on the output from the models for various domain such as science, maths, and other subjects. We needed to design tools such that the annotators could consume instructions and use the utilities such as grammar check, plagiarism detection etc. to complete the tasks in a timely manner. In order to execute the projects and manage the workflows, which are unique to each project, we needed to design a project management tool that allows the project managers to manage the client tasks and projects. The tool also needed to provide insights and analytics on the productivity of the annotators and the quality of the output from the models.

ROLE & RESPONSIBILITY

  • Principal Product Designer / UX Lead for Fine Tune Studio (FTS) products
  • Information architecture and Interaction Design for FTS app and FTS project management tool.
  • Manage design tasks and research of the design team.
  • Problem solving to improve expert productivity and usability of the tools.
  • Hire exceptional designers that meet the technical and product understanding bar as well.
  • Research with crowd workers and experts.

PRIMARY PROJECTS

  • Prompt resonse pair / Conversational chat based reponses
  • Work flow management / Task and job management / Batch management
  • User allocation management
  • Audio annotation tool design
  • Text annotation tool design

OUTCOMES

  • Three column layout with flexible width resulted in significant (30-40%) productivity improvement for annotators as it helped them to consume instructions and use the utilities such as grammar check, plagiarism detection etc. to complete the tasks in a timely manner.
  • Optimised information architecture resulted in productivity improvement and pleasant experience for the project managers.
  • Several UX enhancements resulted in qualitative and productivity improvements

Design of Data Annotations tools for AI use-cases

Playment (Telus International AI Data Solutions)

INTRODUCTION

Data annotation tools are used to annotate or mark 2D and 3D data including images , videos and 3D point clouds. This data is used to train models for ML and AI related use-cases. A blend of creative thinking and logical analysis is often required to solve problems and design solutions. The design for these tools requires tool interaction design principles similar to the likes of tools like Blender and photoshop. As cost of annotation is directly related to man-hours of this business, design objectives are tied to improving annotator productivity and reducting the time to complete annotations.

ROLE & RESPONSIBILITY

  • Principal Product Designer / UX Manager Lead for Data Annotation tools.
  • Interaction Design for LIDAR 3D annotation, Video annotation and Semantic segmentation (2D and 3D) tools.
  • Manage design tasks and research of the design team.
  • Problem solving to improve annotator productivity and usability.
  • Hire exceptional designers that meet the technical and product understanding bar as well.
  • Manage user research with annotators from BPOs and inHouse QAs to understand the problems at the root level.
  • Design support for POCs and new features for sales demos.
  • Create design flows and optimize them to improve productivity.
  • Tech feasibility and solutioning discussions with the dev team.
  • Manage design and research documentation.

PRIMARY PROJECTS

  • Sequential PCS tool design to handle annotations for sequential point cloud data.
  • Design of Aggregate point cloud (APC) annotation flow to improve productivty.
  • Sequential PCS Quality evaluation tool design.
  • Sequential semantic segmentation to handle annotations for video tasks.
  • Grid view feature to improve productivity of Quality Analysts.
  • Timeline UX improvements
  • Linking of Parent-Child classes
  • Nesting of classes

OUTCOMES

  • Time to create cuboid annotationusing APC feature reduced by 2x.
  • Grid view improved QA productivity by 3-4x.
  • Several UX enhancements resulted in qualitative and productivity improvements
